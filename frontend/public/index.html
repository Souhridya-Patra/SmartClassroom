Live Recognition from Video

        
Streams frames from webcam at a fixed interval and forwards detected identities to attendance backend.

        
        
        
        
        

          
          
          
        

        

      


      

        
Recognition Pipeline

        
    
          
  • Face detection: MTCNN for aligned face tensors.
    • 
          
  • Embedding: FaceNet to 512D vector on CUDA when available.
    • 
          
  • Matching: cosine similarity against registered embeddings in memory/file metadata.
    • 
          
  • Formula: cos(theta) = (A dot B) / (|A| * |B|), where A and B are 512D vectors.
    • 
          
  • Decision: similarity > 0.60 is positive identity match.
    • 
          
  • Storage policy: no student photos are persisted by this system.
